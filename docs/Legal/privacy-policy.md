Devvyyxyz ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Xeno Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, stores, and protects your data. By using Xeno Bot, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

Xeno Bot collects the following types of data:

### **Discord User Data**
1. User IDs  
2. Usernames and profile pictures  
3. Commands issued to the Bot  
4. Message content when interacting with the Bot  
5. Custom preferences set by the user  

### **Discord Guild (Server) Data**
1. Guild IDs  
2. Guild names and profile pictures  
3. Bot feature configurations (e.g., command settings or preferences for enabled features)  

!!! note
    Xeno Bot does not store server-wide settings or moderation logs unless directly linked to bot features.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We collect data to:
1. Enable and improve the **Bot’s usability** (e.g., saving preferences or enabling custom server features).  
2. Maintain **statistics for the bot's performance** and usage.  
3. Troubleshoot errors or issues through logging.  
4. Gather **feedback** to improve the Bot.  
5. Ensure a better overall user experience.  

!!! tip
    Data like commands used or logs may be analyzed in aggregate to improve the Bot’s features and performance.

---

## 3. Data Retention and Storage

### Data Retention
- Data collected by Xeno Bot is stored indefinitely to support features like preferences, command statistics, and configurations.  
- If a user or server discontinues the use of Xeno Bot, their data will **not be automatically deleted**. Users may request deletion (see section 5).

### Storage and Protection
- All stored data is securely protected and accessible **only to us** (Devvyyxyz and the development team).  
- Discord services may store some data based on their systems' functionality.

!!! warning
    While we take every precaution to protect your data, users should also be aware of Discord's own privacy policies and guidelines.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

- We do **not share your data** with any third parties.  
- Data collection is strictly limited to improving and operating Xeno Bot.  
- Discord may store certain data (e.g., content sent via messages or interaction metadata) based on their platform systems.

---

## 5. User Rights

At any time, users have the right to:
1. Request to view the data collected about them by Xeno Bot.
2. Request that their data be deleted from our systems.  

To exercise these rights:
- Open a ticket on [our official support server](#add-your-server-invite-link-here).  
- Provide your **user ID** and details about your request (e.g., data deletion or data view request).  

We will respond to these requests within a reasonable timeframe.

!!! note
    Data removal requests are permanent and cannot be undone.

---

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted publicly at the same location. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how their data is handled.

!!! tip
    Continued use of Xeno Bot after changes to the policy constitute agreement to the updated terms.

---

## 7. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us:
- **Support Ticket**: Open a ticket on our official Discord support server.  
- **GitHub Repository**: Raise issues or inquiries via [Xeno Bot's GitHub page](https://github.com/devvyyxyz/xeno-bot).

---

This Privacy Policy is designed to provide transparency and clarity on how your data is handled when using Xeno Bot. If you have concerns, our team is here to assist you.
